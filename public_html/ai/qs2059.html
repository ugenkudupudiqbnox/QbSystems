The Verdict: Own vs. Rent

Break-even within 1 year

By month 12, cumulative cloud spend matches or exceeds the cost of owning the machine. After that, cloud becomes strictly more expensive.

Cloud costs never stop

With cloud, you pay every hour, every semester. Budgeting is unpredictable and costs spike during peak usage (deadlines). With on-prem, usage is unlimited once purchased.

Hidden Cloud Costs

Standard cloud billing often omits high-volume costs that are included in on-prem ownership:

  • Data Egress Fees: Moving large model weights and datasets.
  • Storage Charges: High-performance persistent disks.
  • Idle Billed Instances: Paying for compute during setup or student downtime.
  • Management Overhead: Time spent on account and quota management.
These typically add 10–25% extra over the listed GPU hourly costs.
